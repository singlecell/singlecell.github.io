Professional Summary

Associate Professor at the Institute of Chinese Materia Medica, Shanghai University of Traditional Chinese Medicine. My research develops mass spectrometry, chromatography, and sample-pretreatment technologies for the discovery and precise characterization of active ingredients in natural medicines, endogenous molecular targeting and metabolomics, green chemistry strategies, and the quality control and safety evaluation of natural products.

Research interests

Analytical technology innovation: New mass spectrometry, chromatography, and sample-pretreatment technologies for the precise analysis of complex natural-medicine systems
Active ingredient discovery: Discovery and structural characterization of bioactive constituents in traditional Chinese medicines
Targeting analysis & metabolomics: Endogenous molecular targeting analysis and metabolomics to unravel mechanisms of action
Quality control & safety: Green-chemistry strategies plus the quality control and safety evaluation of natural products
